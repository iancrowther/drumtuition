Professional Drum Teacher

Chiswick, London

I have been a professional drummer for 35 years and now focus my time teaching.

I teach all ages and standards of drummer from beginner to advanced on a one to one basis in my home studio. I can teach you to work with the clicker or sequencers and am happy to help you develop your own material. I can help you along with gradings from Trinty Rockschool or Guildhall from Grade 1 to 8. I also teach one to one and small classes in schools and colleges.
