I am a professional drummer with over 35 years experience and am now passing on that experience by providing drum lessons to students from all around the world. My studio in Chiswick, West London, has turned out some top professional drummers as well as helping many students to explore the drums as a hobby.

Whatever your age, musical background, or drumming experience, you have come to right tutor - I can teach you from the moment you pick up your drumsticks for the first time to helping you, as an experienced or professional drummer, to fine tune your skills. We will work together in my studio on a one to one basis, in lessons specifically tailored to your development, your way, to achieve your objectives. You will be taught as an individual and our sessions will be fun. I teach because I love watching seeing students develop and get the best out of themselves.

You can learn drums with the clicker or sequencers, and I'm happy to help you develop your own material. I can help you along with gradings from Trinty Rockschool or Guildhall from Grade 1 to 8. Most Styles Covered Including: Jazz, Latin, Rock, Funk & Fusion.

I hold a current CRB and give group and individual drumming lessons in schools and colleges. References are available upon request.

For more information and to discuss rates and package deals please contact me by phone or email and I'll get back to you within 24hrs. Get in touch today and learn to play the drums.
